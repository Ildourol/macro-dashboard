How to read: The doughnut splits E-mini S&P option spread volume by structure type. Verticals and ratio spreads are defined-risk directional bets; condors/butterflies are range bets; straddles/strangles are volatility bets. Use: A vertical-heavy book confirms the compressed-vol regime: professionals are financing directional views instead of paying full premium - mimic that (debit spreads over outrights) while VIX stays sub-16.
