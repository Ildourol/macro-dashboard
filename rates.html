4. Implied Policy Path — SOFR Strip FACT (settlements, 07/02/2026) + skill-derived calculation

Methodology (macro-rates-monitor skill): Each SOFR futures settlement implies a forward 3-month rate: implied rate = 100 - settle price. Reading the strip month-by-month converts the futures board the market actually trades into an explicit policy path — no survey, no dots, just cleared risk.
