3. FX Options Call/Put Split by Currency

How to read: Paired bars show call vs. put notional per currency (log scale - EUR/JPY dwarf the rest, so small currencies stay visible). Green above red = market paying for that currency to strengthen vs. USD; red above green = paying for downside protection. Current read: EUR and JPY are call-heavy (61/39 and 59/41) while GBP is extremely put-heavy (20/80) - the options market is funding the anti-dollar squeeze in the majors while hedging sterling. Use: Follow the convexity: option skew often leads the futures move because it is where informed traders position cheaply first.
